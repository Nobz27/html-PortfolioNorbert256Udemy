Top 10 Action Movies of All Time

  1. The Dark Knight

    • Why it ranks high: Powerful villain, intense action, legendary performance.

  2. Mad Max: Fury Road

    • Non-stop action from beginning to end.

  3. Terminator 2: Judgment Day

    • Revolutionary special effects + emotional story.

  4. Die Hard

    • The movie that defined modern action cinema.

  5. John Wick

    • Clean gun-fu choreography and stylish combat.

  6. Gladiator

    • Epic historical action with powerful storytelling.

  7. The Matrix

    • Changed action filmmaking forever.

  8. The Raid

    • One of the best martial arts action films ever made.

  9. Mission: Impossible – Fallout

    • Real stunts, intense pacing.

  10. Avengers: Endgame

    • Massive superhero action spectacle.
